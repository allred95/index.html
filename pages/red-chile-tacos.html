if your looking for a quick an easy meal that the whole family will enjoy? try this twist on the classic street taco!

for this dish you will need

    1 pound boneless skinless chicken thighs

    Solive oil

    salt

    pepper

    1 pouch of Frontera red chile enchilada sauce (or similar)

    one 14 ounce can pinto beans (rinsed and drained)

    1/4 cup mayo

    2 tablespoons cream cheese

    2 cups frozen or fresh sweet corn

    1 clove garlic, grated

    1/2 teaspoon salt

    1 teaspoon cumin

    1 teaspoon chili powder

    lime juice to taste

    1 red onion, thinly sliced

    1/2 cup red wine vinegar

    1/2 cup water

    1 teaspoon salt

    1 tablespoon sugar

    corn tortillas (I like to quickly pan-fry mine in a shallow skillet of hot oil to make them extra soft)

    cilantro
